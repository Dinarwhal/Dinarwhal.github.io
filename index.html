Pocket Doc is a mobile web app that enables users to quickly retrieve all of the information needed to complete doctors' forms and answer questions about personal and family medical history. Users will create an account unique to them and input personal history such as: vaccines and shots they have had, any allergies they might have, and medicines and medications they take or have taken before, as well as keep track of doctors' appointments using a calendar.
Pocket Doc is unique because users have complete control over how much information they would like to keep in the app. In the future, Pocket Doc will have a feature that enables families to share as much or as little information with each other using a passcode specific to the family. This feature is extremely helpful for parents who have children who like to keep track of their own medical history and for children who are in college.
